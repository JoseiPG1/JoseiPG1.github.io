--- layout: archive title: "How to build the action?" permalink: /notes.html author_profile: true --- In order to build up the action, first we decomposed the affine connection $\Gamma$ into its irreducible fields \begin{equation} \begin{aligned} \label{affine_connection} \hat{\Gamma}_{\alpha}{}^{\beta}{}_{\gamma} & = \hat{\Gamma}_{(\alpha}{}^{\beta}{}_{\gamma)} + \hat{\Gamma}_{[\alpha}{}^{\beta}{}_{\gamma]}, \\ & = \ctG{\alpha}{\beta}{\gamma} + \B{\alpha}{\beta}{\gamma} + \delta^{\beta}_{[\gamma}\A{\alpha]}, \end{aligned} \end{equation}
